Warm Tasty Beef Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made up of layers of pasta, meat or vegetables, sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of beef broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 9-12 lasagna sheets
  • 500g ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 450g shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 45g of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, brown the ground beef over medium heat, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  2. Add the onion and garlic to the pan and cook until the onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes, broth, tomato paste, basil, and oregano. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the sauce to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the flavors have melded and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, eggs, and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  8. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Arrange 3-4 lasagna sheets over the sauce, breaking them to fit if necessary.
  10. Spoon half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, spreading it evenly.
  11. Repeat the layers of sauce, noodles, and ricotta mixture.
  12. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  13. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Allow the lasagna to cool for 10-15 minutes before serving.

Enjoy! This lasagna recipe can be easily adapted to your taste preferences by using different meats, adding vegetables such as spinach or zucchini, or using different cheeses.
